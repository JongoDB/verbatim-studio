# Your First Transcription

## Quick Answer
Upload an audio or video file to the Recordings page, click Transcribe, and wait for processing to complete. Then click the recording to view and edit the transcript.

## Step-by-Step

1. Navigate to **Recordings** in the sidebar
2. Drag and drop your audio/video file onto the page
   - Or click the **Upload** button to browse files
3. Enter a title for the recording
4. Click **Transcribe** to start processing
5. Wait for the status to change to "Completed"
   - Small files take seconds, large files may take longer
6. Click the recording to open the transcript view
7. Play audio and follow along with the transcript

## UI Location
- **Page**: Recordings
- **Key buttons**: Upload, Transcribe
- **Status indicator**: Shows pending, processing, completed, or failed

## Tips
- Enable "Auto-transcribe" in Settings to skip step 4
- Start with a short file (under 5 minutes) to test
- If transcription is slow, try a smaller model in Settings > Transcription
- The first transcription may take longer while models load

## What's Next?
After your first transcription, you can:
- Edit the transcript text
- Identify and name speakers
- Highlight important segments
- Export to different formats
- Ask the AI assistant questions about the content
