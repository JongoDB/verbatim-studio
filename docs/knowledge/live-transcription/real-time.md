# Live Real-Time Transcription

## Quick Answer
The Live page lets you transcribe speech in real-time using your microphone. Click Connect, speak, and see words appear as you talk. Save the session as a recording when done.

## Step-by-Step

1. Navigate to **Live** in the sidebar
2. Click **Connect**
3. Allow microphone access when prompted
4. Select your language
5. Start speaking - transcription appears in real-time
6. Click **Stop** when finished
7. Click **Save** to convert to a recording

## UI Location
- **Page**: Live
- **Key buttons**: Connect, Stop, Save
- **Language selector**: Choose source language
- **Transcript area**: Shows real-time text

## Requirements
- Working microphone
- Browser permission for microphone access
- AI models downloaded (Settings > Transcription)

## Tips
- Speak clearly and at a moderate pace
- Reduce background noise for better accuracy
- The live transcript may have more errors than post-processing
- Saved sessions can be re-transcribed for higher accuracy
- A session timer shows elapsed time

## Troubleshooting

### Microphone not detected
- Check system audio settings
- Try a different browser
- Ensure no other app is using the microphone

### No transcription appearing
- Check that models are downloaded
- Verify microphone permission was granted
- Try disconnecting and reconnecting

## Note
Live transcription is in **BETA**. Accuracy may be lower than file-based transcription.
